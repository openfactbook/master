Guam
====

(territory of the US)


Introduction

  Background:
    Spain ceded Guam to the US in 1898. Captured by the Japanese in 1941,
    it was retaken by the US three years later. The military installations
    on the island are some of the most strategically important US bases in
    the Pacific.
